Text Extraction

Text extraction using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is a process of converting different types of documents, such as scanned paper documents, PDF files, or images captured by a digital camera, into editable and searchable data. OCR technology identifies text within these documents or images and converts it into machine-readable text.

Text Summarization

Text summarization using NLP (Natural Language Processing) involves condensing a large piece of text into a shorter version while retaining the most important information. There are generally two approaches to text summarization: extractive and abstractive. Text summarization using NLP has numerous applications, including news summarization

Text To Speech

GTTS or Google Text-to-Speech,is a Python library and API developed by Google that converts text into spoken words.It utilizes Google's Text-to -Speech service,which offers high-quality,natural-sounding speech synthesis in multiple languages and accents GTTS is a simple and convenient tool for adding text-to-speech functionality
